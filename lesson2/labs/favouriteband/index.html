NETSKY

Introduction

Presenting a lone kid out of Belgium throwing down some sweet beats with his own mix of Liquid Funk, Drum and Bass and great vocal tracks.

In late 2009, Netsky signed a recording contract with Hospital Records, the same label as other well known liquid artists such as High Contrast and London Elektricity. In less than 12 months, the Belgian musician secured releases with record labels including Liq-Weed Ganja, Spearhead and Talkin Beatz. He also won a nomination as "Best Upcoming Producer" within just months of his first release at the Drum + Bass Arena Awards.

Meet the guy who is dragging the electronic movement into the mainstream kicking and screaming. Watch his latest music video 'Puppy' from latest album 2 Deluxe and see his next stop on his upcoming tour!
